Time in Our Space

" Time is not a line but a dimension, like the dimensions of space. "
- Margaret Atwood

Time is a system of measurement that we, as humans, constructed. Our living experiences has always been defined by the very hours, minutes, and seconds.

Rather than experiencing time through numbers, let's experience time through events that occurs in our space of existence.

On the Earth (green sphere) , for every:

Second - A radio wave is sent out into the solar system from Earth (gray line)

Minute - An earthquake happens on Earth's crust (brown triangle)

Hour - The Space Station travels 17,500 miles around Earth (beige rectangle)
